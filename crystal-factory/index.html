Inside Our Workshops

Every crystal bracelet, tower, and sphere passes through several high-precision workshops in our factory before being packed for export.

1. Raw Stone Classification

Our raw material warehouse stores tons of high-grade raw crystal minerals directly shipped from Brazil, Madagascar, and Mozambique. Our specialists inspect every block of rose quartz, amethyst, and obsidian for purity and color density.

2. Carving & Bead Drilling

Utilizing high-end drilling machinery, our beads workshop produces extremely round beads from 4mm to 14mm with perfect concentric holes, avoiding sharp internal edges that would cause cord snapping in retail shops.

3. Hand-Polishing & Detailing

Crystal towers, obelisks, and intricate custom shape carvings (angels, skulls, animals) are hand-polished by our veteran artisans using diamond-infused polishing wheels to achieve a high-end glassy sheen.

🌱 Our Pledge: 100% Natural Minerals & Ethical Sourcing

As an industry-leading crystal manufacturer, we take environmental sustainability and human rights extremely seriously. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy against child labor or exploitation in our mine-sourcing partners. All raw gems are imported under legally compliant channels with full customs declarations.

★ Certificate of Mineral Authenticity: Every bulk order is eligible for a Gemstone Testing Report certified by China Gemological Institute (GIC / NGTC) to prove 100% natural, unheated, and untreated mineral properties.
